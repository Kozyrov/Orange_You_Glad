Boxes of Fruit


Problem: There are three boxes of fruit. One box is full of oranges only. A second box is full of apples only. A third box is a random mix of both. But, all three boxes are mislabelled. For example, the box labelled "Apples" may very well be only oranges, or have both Oranges and Apples.

Solution: Determine the correct contents of all three boxes by recealing one fruit only from one box only. If you coose correctly, you will be able to determine the correct contents of all three boxes.
Hint: the boxes are mislabelled, so remember that the "apples" box cannot contain apples.
